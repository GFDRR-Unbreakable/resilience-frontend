Mitigating losses and helping people escape from poverty

$1 in disaster loss does not mean the same thing to a rich person as it does to a poor person. The Unbreakable Resilience Indicator measures socioeconomic resilience by combining risk to assets and risk to well-being. It measures not only the total losses but also how these losses translates into income losses to better design policies that protect the most vulnerable.
