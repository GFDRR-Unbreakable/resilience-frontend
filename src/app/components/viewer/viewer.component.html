Risk to assets (% of GDP) is the value of assets at risk, considering hazard exposure and asset vulnerability.

Socioeconomic resilience: The ability of the population to recover from disasters (the ratio of asset losses to well-being losses).

Risk to well-being (% of GDP) is the potential impact of asset losses on consumption.
